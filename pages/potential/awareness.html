Awareness

Most of us are never fully aware of our potential. From a very young age, we are told what we can do and what we cannot. When curious kids question us or ask way too many why’s, we do need their curiosity. Instead, following the traditional parenting system, we tell them not not to ask for reasons.

This is where Korroboree helps children, parents, teachers and caregivers to understand how to embrace a growth mindset and develop an intent to expand our potential to the fullest. When you onboard a journey with us, be ready to experience a whole new world of parenting where being unique is rewarding and low-scoring grades are not frowned upon.

Korroboree believes in being self-aware. The moment we reach a state of complete self awareness is when we learn to appreciate efforts more than results. We understand that our potential is infinite and that we can nurture to grow into the best versions of ourselves.

Once we are aware of our potential is when we can work towards new-age parenting where we raise children who are ready to face the uncertainties, challenges and downfalls that life throws at us and spring back from adversities every time.
