WHO WE ARE

A brainchild of Mr. Agnelorajesh Athaide and Ms. Lina Ashar, Korroboree is an initiative to spread the word about a newer way of parenting. We reach out to those who shape the minds of the young ones- parents, primary caregivers, and teachers. You can explore several unconventional ways in which you empower your child to unleash their full potential. With access to tools and resources, Korroboree helps you to understand how neuroscience, behavioural science and energy science, motivational science, and developing habits of mind works in making a child use any obstacle or downfall in life as a springboard to bounce back to life.

Every parent has their own way of raising a child. However, what is common in most of the cases is the fixed mindset. Parents measure their child’s success by their marks, grades, medals, and awards. The mission is an expansion of thinking, mindful contemplation, and a shift from parenting for our children to survive, to parenting for our children to thrive, and in the process of creating a sustainable, peaceful, and abundant world.

Values

Korroboree commits to making a healthy environment for young minds where parents, caregivers, and teachers ensure an all-round physical, mental and emotional development.

Vision

We envision a world where we encourage, embrace, and engage in mindful parenting to raise mentally emotionally healthy children.
Mission

We strive to make a shift from competing to collaboration, from inculcating finite potential to discovering infinite potential where children are raised with mindful parenting.

Our Thought Leaders

Korroboree was born when Lina Ashar, the leading edupreneur, joined hands with Agnelorajesh Athaide, a zealous entrepreneur, business strategist, and educationalist. It is an initiative committed to ‘Creating the Best Version of Myself’. Both Lina and Agnelorajesh share the same passion for changing the way the existing education system and conventional parenting limit a child’s curiosity and creativity.

Through Korroboree, parents and teachers who shape young minds can maximise their potential to the fullest and thereby set examples to children whose mind, heart and spirit embrace the growth mindset, mindfulness, and optimism.
