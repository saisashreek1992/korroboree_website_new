WHAT SHE DO

A small river named Duden flows by their place and supplies it with the necessary regelialia.


A teacher at heart, Lina Ashar has always been super enthusiastic when it comes to crafting a model of education that is fulfilling, effective and yet stress-free. With her primary education completed in England, Lina moved to Australia, where she acquired a bachelor’s degree in Education from Victoria College in Melbourne. In 1991, when Lina came on a year long sabbatical from college, the Indian education system appalled her. At that time schools were not child-centric but focused entirely on books, marks, exams and grades leading to disinterested students and over-stressed parents.

Changing the system was no one-day task. Lina’s first step towards today’s massive success started with a preschool in Bandra (Mumbai) in the year 1993 with 25 students, where education was offered with the child at the centre. Today we have a network of Kangaroo Kids Preschool and Billabong High International schools spread across more than 44 cities in India and also across Dubai, Maldives and Qatar.

Since then, she has taken the initiative to change the fixed mindset of primary caregivers that tend to limit a child’s potential. Lina co-founded Korroboree to help every parent, caregiver, and teacher learn and inculcate qualities for the expansion of human potential and human consciousness. She strongly believes that grades and marks secured in board examinations can never foretell a successful future. Lina has worked with several like-minded experts who have helped her in her initiative to create a portal where you can learn about parenting and expand your potential to the fullest. Her zeal and passion for creating a better world for our future generations have earned her several awards and accolades from all over the world. An inspiration for people of all ages, Lina is trusted by parents and loved by children she works with
